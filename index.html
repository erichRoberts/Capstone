Dividing the Camels

A man travelling through the desert came across 3 brothers arguing. Their father had died and left them his herd of camels, with strict instructions about how to divide them.

  • The eldest to receive 1/2 of the herd
  • The middle brother to receive 1/3 of the herd
  • The youngest to receive 1/9 of the herd

But there were 17 camels, and that meant the division was impossible

The man offered to donate his camel to solve the problem. There were now 18 camels.

  • 1/2 of the herd was 9 camels
  • 1/3 of the herd was 6 camels
  • 1/9 of the herd was 2 camels

This was total of 17 camels, with one to spare. The will of the father had been perfectly fulfilled! The man took back his camel that was no longer needed, and headed off on his journey.

This is what a skilled facilitor can do in even the most difficult situation.
